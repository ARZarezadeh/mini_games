## If your goal is specifically to become a stronger programmer

I would do these in order:

1. Tic-Tac-Toe
2. Blackjack
3. Battleship
4. Connect Four
5. Poker Hand Evaluator
6. Maze Generator + Solver
7. Sudoku Solver
8. Minesweeper
9. RPG Battle System
10. Trading Card Game Engine

That path will expose you to:

* OOP
* Recursion
* Graph algorithms
* Backtracking
* AI search (Minimax)
* Event-driven architecture
* Clean code organization

Those are skills that transfer directly to professional development, including Odoo backend work.
